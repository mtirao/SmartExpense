SmartExpense Help

The purpose of this app is to integrate all other application from ArgSoft Solutions, such as SmartMoney, SmartList, SmartFuel in this way you will have all the information gathered in one place.

If you take a look to the Management menu, you will see three different sections, one for SmartMoney, the next one os for all SmartList related task and the last one for SmartFuel. Below, we will describe each command.

SmartMoney

Bank

To start working with SmartMoney you need to add a Bank, to do this follow this steps

  • Goto to Managment > New Bank...
  • Click on the little + button on the bottom left of the window
  • Type in on all fields on the with desired information

To delete a bank just selecte the one you want to delete and click the little - button on the bottom left of the window

To modify a bank information just select the one you want to modify and type the new using the sama procedure as you do when you add a new bank. It's also posible by double click on the list the field you want to modify

Bank Account

Once you have added a Bank, you can now add an account information, to do this follow this steps

  • Goto to Management > New Bank Account...
  • Click on the little + button on the bottom left of the window
  • Type in on all fields on the with desired information

To delete or modify an account just follow the same step described in the Bank Section

Expense

After completing all previous step, you can now add an expense to this follow the step below

  • Goto to Management > Expenses...
  • Click on the little + button on the bottom left of the window
  • Type in on all fields on the with desired information

To delete or modify an account just follow the same step described in the Bank Section

Schedule Expense

This option let's you add recurring expenses

  • Goto to Management > Scheduel Expenses...
  • Click on the little + button on the bottom left of the window
  • Type in on all fields on the with desired information
